About Me

I was born and raised in South Los Angeles, California. I began my career as a tutor for college-bound students in a local charter school and later became a Resource Specialist Para-educator certified through the Los Angeles Unified School District. Eventually, I worked in education for ten years providing 1-on-1 instruction to students with special needs.

It was a great experience to see my student’s academic achievements in the time I spent working with them, but I felt that I needed a new challenge for my own personal growth. The opportunity arose to take a huge risk, move across the country, and dare to dream a new dream. I moved to Durham, North Carolina and attained certification as a Full Stack Web Developer from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill’s Coding Program Boot Camp in hopes of broadening my skills and exploring new career opportunities.

While moving across the country to pursue a new career has been far from easy, I know that my positive attitude combined with my hard-work ethic will enable me to overcome whatever obstacles life throws my way.
